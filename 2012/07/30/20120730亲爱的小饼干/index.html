亲爱的小饼干

I dedicate this poem to my wife and son for their continuous support to my work.
我把这首诗献给我的太太和儿子，感谢他们对我的工作的持续不断的支持。

Acknowledgement to Chinese Poet KE Yan and British Poet William Wordsworth.
向中国诗人柯岩和英国诗人威廉华兹华斯致敬！

The Chinese version is listed in the later part of this poem.
中文版本列于本电邮的后半部分。

DEAR BISCUIT, WHERE ARE YOU?

Dear Biscuit, my beloved sweetheart
Where are you? Ah, where are you?
Don’t you know that I am missing you?
AJI staff is missing you

I shouted out to Lotus Hill
“Dear Biscuit …”
The Hill echoed
“She has just gone, just gone
She is on the way to a client’s meeting
She is marching along Fuhua Yi Road, never taking a break.”

I shouted out to the City Plaza
“Dear Biscuit…”
The long-span roof replied
“She has just gone, just gone
Don’t you see the finished drawings in the Transfer Drive?
Her eyes were red with exhaustion after a sleepless night of overtime.”

I shouted to the lift lobby
“Dear Biscuit …”
The arcade murmured
“She has just gone, just gone
She was busy in preparing a report just now.”

I searched for you all over the city
“Dear Biscuit …”
You have been expanding your footprints
In every corner of the City

I returned to our office
And shouted again
“Dear Biscuit …”
The Sectary answered
“Keep quiet, keep quiet
She is discussing with our Boss about something important.”

Ah, Dear Biscuit, my beloved sweetheart
You are right here, right here
My dear, dear sister
You are just in my heart
In our hearts
This prayer I make
That neither rumor nor greed
Can hurt you

Will you forget
That we stood together in the sunshine of those delightful afternoons
And that I, a worshiper of the Total Design Ideal
Work here with warmer love
With far deeper zeal of holier love
Nor will you forget
That after many days of absence
AJI Office is to me
More dear, both for itself and for your sake!

Best Regards,

XXX

30/07/2012

亲爱的小饼干，你在哪里？

亲爱的小饼干，我的甜心
你在哪里啊，你在哪里？
你是否知道我在想你
阿记员工在想你

我对着莲花山喊
“亲爱的小饼干……”
山丘回响
“她刚离去，刚离去
她在赶往与业主见面的路上
她正行进在福华一路的大道上，毫不停息。”

我向城市广场大声喊
“亲爱的小饼干……”
大跨天棚回应着
“她刚离去，刚离去
你没有看到传送盘里刚完成的图纸文件么？
经历了一个无眠的加班夜，她眼睛红红的充满了疲惫。”

我向电梯大堂喊
“亲爱的小饼干……”
拱廊轻声呢哝
“她刚离去，刚离去
刚才她还正忙着准备一份报告。”

我在整个城市找寻你
“亲爱的小饼干……”
你的足迹遍布这个城市的
每个角落

我回到我们的办公室
再次大声呼喊
“亲爱的小饼干……”
秘书回答
“细声点啦，细声点
佢而家正同老细倾重要的事情。”

啊，亲爱的小饼干，我亲爱的甜心
你在这里，在这里
我亲而又亲的妹妹
你原来就在我的心里
在我们心里
我在此祈祷
无论流言蜚语还是贪心
都不会伤害到你

你不会忘记在那些快乐午后的阳光里
我们曾并肩站在一起
不会忘记， 我， 一体化设计理念的崇拜者
在这里工作　怀着更深的爱
怀着更神圣的爱
也不会忘记
经过了多天的离别
阿记办公室对我来说
显得更加亲切， 半因为它自己　半因为你的缘故！

祝顺！

XXX
